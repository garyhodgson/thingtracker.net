Q & A

The information below attempts to anticipate questions that may arise whilst considering and discussing the Thing tracker Network. If any question is not shown below, and you feel it would aid the discussion, then feel free to get in touch.

Note: this page is not yet complete.

General

What is a tracker?
talk about place to submit information about 3d printing designs.
Where do I host my files?
- explain why the tracker does not host files - suggest github, personal hosting, reprap wiki, physibleexchange, thingiverse
What's to stop spam being entered in the network?
- network of trust & spammy trackers will be ignored
Why should I login?
- ability to edit/remove your thing submission.
What personal data is stored in the tracker?
- none. email and oauth id used to hash an id to track submissions (for editing and deleting purposes)
What rights do I forfeit by adding my project/thing to a tracker?
Talk about distribution of information in the network
